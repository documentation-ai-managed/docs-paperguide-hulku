## Overview

Spaces help you organize documents by project or team.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Dashboard">
    Log into Paperguide-hulku and go to your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Space'">
    Find and click the "New Space" button at the top-right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Space Name">
    Enter a name and optional description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click "Create" to finish. Your new space is ready for documents and folders.
  </Step>
</Steps>
