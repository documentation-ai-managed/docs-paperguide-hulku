---
title: Cannot Add New Document
description: Fix issues adding documents in Paperguide-hulku.
---

## Symptoms

- The "Add Document" button is disabled
- Error: "Unable to create document"

## Causes

- Missing permissions
- Space or folder is set to read-only

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Permissions">
    Ask your admin for edit access in your space settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Space/Folders">
    Make sure you're in a writable space or folder (not archived or locked).
  </Step>
</Steps>
