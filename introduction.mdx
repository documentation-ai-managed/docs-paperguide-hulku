## Overview

Paperguide empowers researchers, students, and professionals with AI-driven tools to discover relevant papers, conduct literature reviews, generate insights, and organize references seamlessly. You streamline your workflow from search to writing, saving hours on manual tasks. Access powerful features like AI search and writing assistance directly from your browser.
