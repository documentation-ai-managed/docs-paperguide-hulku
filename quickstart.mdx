## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Ensure you have:
- A valid email address for account verification
- Access to your email inbox for confirmation links
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
</Callout>

Paperguide requires no downloads or installations. You start directly in your browser at [https://paperguide.ai](https://paperguide.ai).

## Create and Verify Your Account

Follow these steps to sign up quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Sign-Up Page" icon="globe">
    Navigate to [https://paperguide.ai](https://paperguide.ai) and click **Get Started** or **Sign Up**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email address, create a strong password, and optionally add your name or affiliation.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Your Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Paperguide. Click the link to activate your account.
    
    If you do not see the email, check your spam folder.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Log In and Dashboard Tour

Once verified, log in to access your personalized dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to [https://paperguide.ai](https://paperguide.ai), enter your credentials, and click **Log In**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore the Dashboard" icon="layout-dashboard">
    Familiarize yourself with key areas:
    
    - **AI Search Bar**: Top center for queries
    - **Recent Searches**: Sidebar for history
    - **Literature Review**: Tools for analysis
    - **References**: Organizer panel
    
    Use the navigation menu to switch between features like AI Writer and Reference Manager.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Expandable title="Dashboard Navigation Tips" default-open="false">
  The dashboard adapts to your workflow. Pin frequent searches and customize views via the settings icon in the top right.
</Expandable>

## Run Your First AI Search

Test Paperguide's AI-powered search with a real query.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Academic Research" icon="book-open">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Enter Query" icon="search">
        In the AI Search bar, type: "Recent advances in machine learning for drug discovery"
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Review Results" icon="list">
        Paperguide returns relevant papers with summaries, citations, and AI insights.
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Analyze Further" icon="zap">
        Click **Literature Review** to generate a synthesis or export references.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Clinical Trials" icon="activity">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Enter Query" icon="search">
        Try: "Protocol designs for phase II oncology trials 2023-2024"
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Refine Results" icon="filter">
        Use filters for publication date, authors, or journals.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="success">
Your first search completes in seconds, delivering curated research papers with AI summaries.
</Callout>
